Squad-CAM

We have developed an explanation method built upon Grad-CAM. The method explains why an AI has made the prediction that it made. It allows us to understand why complicated deep learning methods behave in the way that they do. This website compares three different explanation methods (Guided Backpropagation, Guided Grad-CAM and Guided Squad-CAM). The aim is to see which explanation provides the most intuative explanation for humans.

Comparisons:
